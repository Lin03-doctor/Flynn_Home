D-GARA: A Dynamic Benchmarking Framework for GUI Agent Robustness in Real-World Anomalies
Sen Chen, Tong Zhao*, Yi Bin*, Fei Ma, Wenqi Shao, Zheng Wang (* equal contribution, † corresponding author)
Proceedings of the 40th AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI) 2026 ACCEPTED
D-GARA introduces a dynamic benchmark for evaluating Android GUI agents under real-world anomalous conditions such as permission prompts and battery warnings. It combines context-aware anomaly injection with XML-based target-state validation to expose robustness gaps in state-of-the-art agents.
